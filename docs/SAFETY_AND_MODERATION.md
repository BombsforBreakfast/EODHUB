# Safety and Moderation

EOD-HUB supports both admin moderation and personal safety controls for user-generated content.

## Reporting Objectionable Content

Users can report objectionable posts, comments, and messages where the report action is available in the product UI. Reports are sent to the EOD-HUB admin moderation workflow for review.

Reporting is an admin moderation signal. Admins may review the reported content, hide content from the broader community, or take other moderation action when appropriate.

## Hide/Block User

Users can also choose **Hide/Block User** from supported user, post, comment, and messaging surfaces.

A personal block:

- Hides or suppresses that user's posts, comments, messages, notifications, and discovery appearances from the blocker where practical.
- Only affects the blocker’s own EOD-HUB experience.
- Does not ban, suspend, de-verify, or otherwise restrict the blocked user's account globally.
- Does not affect what other users can see.
- Does not notify the blocked user.

Blocked users can be managed from:

**Settings → Privacy & Safety → Blocked Users**

From there, users can unblock someone. After unblocking, they may begin seeing that user's posts, comments, and messages again.

## Separation Between Blocks and Reports

Personal blocks and admin reports are intentionally separate:

- **Hide/Block User** is a private visibility and interaction control for one user.
- **Report Post/Comment/Message** sends content to admin review.
- A personal block does not add the blocked user to an admin queue unless the blocker also submits a report.

This distinction helps protect user safety while avoiding global moderation actions based only on one person's personal visibility preferences.
